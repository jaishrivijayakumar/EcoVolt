The Crisis

EV batteries are a ticking environmental problem

As electric vehicles go mainstream, millions of battery packs are approaching end-of-life. Without proper recycling, these batteries leak toxic heavy metals into soil and water — threatening ecosystems and public health.

☣️

Toxic Material Leaching

Lithium, cobalt, and nickel corrode into groundwater when batteries are landfilled, contaminating drinking water for communities.

🔥

Fire Hazard

Degraded lithium-ion cells are prone to thermal runaway — causing intense, hard-to-extinguish fires in landfills and scrapyards.
